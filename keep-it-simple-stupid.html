Keep it Simple, Stupid!

Tue 21 January 2014

After several days working on it, here it is! Pelican !! So, why I use an static blog instead of a popular blog system? 'cause I love GitHub and I love use Vim as text editor(not IDE) and the simplicity of an static blog is awesome. Work with static blogs reminds me my humble origings, where there's not Bootstrap, or Django or the allmighty PyCharm. Just HTML, reStructuredText and a few commands makes you feel like God, because you always know how your blog works.
