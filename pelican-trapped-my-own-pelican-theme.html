Pelican-Trapped: My own pelican theme

When I decided make my own blog on github, I made a research about all static site generators available. My first choice was Octopress, but I don't speak Ruby yet, so Pelican was introduced to myself by my friend XR09. Written in Python, command line, RST and MarkDown, it was difficult not falling in love with Pelican. Then I face another problem: choose a minimalistic theme that fit with my character.

When I saw Octopress default theme, I choose it without hesitate, because is very simple and readable. But XR09 was a step forward :(. So I decided make my own pelican theme, and here it is! Inspired on pelican-bootstrap3 and simple-bootstrap, and some other ideas I saw around the web.

Feel free to fork the repository and send a pull request.
