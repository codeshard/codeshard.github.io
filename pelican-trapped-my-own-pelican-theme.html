Pelican-Trapped: My own pelican theme

Posted on Mon 24 March 2014 by Ozkar L. Garcell

When I decided make my own blog on github, I made a research about all static site generators available. My first choice was Octopress, but I don't speak Ruby yet, so Pelican was introduced to myself by my friend XR09. Written in Python, command line, RST and MarkDown, it was difficult not falling inlove with Pelican. Then come other problem: choose a minimalistic theme that fit with my character.

When I see Octopress default theme, I choose it without regrets, because is very simple and readable. But XR09 was a step forward :(. So I decided make my own pelican theme, and here is it! Inspired on pelican-bootstrap3 and simple-bootstrap
